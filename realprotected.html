In this page, we will go over the basics of the BloxyLeaks Staff Team.

About

So what is the staff team, you might ask? The BloxyLeaks staff team is comprised of various departments, including moderation, administration, council, management, and ownership. The council department usually oversees and manages all staff duties, making it simpler and more efficient for everyone.

Ranks

Moderation - Manages moderation in the server.

Administration - Administrators of the server.

Council Department - Manages the staff team.

Management - Manages the server.

Ownership - Owners of the server.
